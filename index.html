About me

I am a Research Assistant in the department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, University of Houston, under the guidance of Dr. Jiefu Chen, UH MODAL LAB. My Ph.D. research focuses on using deep learning to improve electromagnetic forward and inverse modeling, and geophysical joint imaging.
